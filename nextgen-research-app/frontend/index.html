What is happening under the hood?

1. Scoping your question The assistant first makes sure it really understands what you want. For very broad or vague questions, it may internally refine or expand the brief.
2. Researching with tools It searches the web (and optionally your documents) in several rounds, writing down notes and checking whether it has enough evidence.
3. Supervising and combining findings For complex topics, a supervisor agent coordinates multiple sub-researchers, each focused on a sub-topic, and then merges their notes.
4. Writing a clear report Finally, a writer model turns the brief and notes into a human-readable report you can copy or download.
Tip: start with one concrete question. If the answer is useful, you can ask follow-up questions or try a different preset mode.
